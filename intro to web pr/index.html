Quick Recipes

Breakfast

20 min

Foul Mudammas :

Instructions: Quick Foul Mudammas: - Drain and rinse canned fava beans. - Sauté minced garlic in olive oil. Add fava beans. - Partially mash beans, season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. - Spoon onto a plate or in a bowl. - Top with tomatoes, cucumbers, radishes, and parsley. Sprinkle cumin. - Serve with flatbread or pita. Enjoy your quick and tasty Foul Mudammas breakfast!

Lunch

35 min

Chicken Shawarma Wrap :

Instructions:Marinate thin chicken slices with garlic, yogurt, olive oil, and shawarma spices. Cook marinated chicken in a hot pan until fully cooked. Mix tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and a pinch of salt for a quick tahini sauce. Spread tahini sauce on a flatbread. Add cooked chicken, sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, and fresh parsley. Roll into a wrap and serve with a side of pickles or a salad. Enjoy your quick and tasty Lebanese Chicken Shawarma Wrap for lunch!

Dinner

45 min

Lebanese Grilled Kebabs :

Instructions:Marinate skewered chicken or beef with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and Middle Eastern spices. Grill the kebabs until fully cooked and slightly charred. Prepare a quick Tzatziki sauce by mixing yogurt, cucumber, garlic, dill, and a squeeze of lemon. Serve the grilled kebabs on a bed of rice or flatbread. Top with Tzatziki sauce and garnish with chopped parsley. Pair with a side salad or roasted vegetables for a complete dinner. Enjoy your quick and flavorful Lebanese Grilled Kebabs with Tzatziki for dinner!
